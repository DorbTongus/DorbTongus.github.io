Working With Feather the Nest

The Process and the Promise

Our journey together begins with a consultation in your space to determine your goals, scope of work and budget. I’ll ask you a lot of questions about what you like/don’t like about the space, what inspires you, and what your goals are. If you have a mood board or some inspirational photos or clippings, great! I’d love to see them.

Once we’ve determined our direction, I’ll create a mood board for your approval and discuss how we move forward, and how the project will be staged.

Whether you are simply looking for advice on a colour scheme or planning a major over-haul, I’ll guide you through each step and supervise crews working in your space. I’ll work with your contractor or bring in a team of my own.

I specialize in making spaces personal and special. If all you need is someone to add some charm to your existing interior, I can help. A hunter/gather of beautiful things, I know where to find one-of-a-kind and unusual pieces and works of art that will help make your home unique.

Nuts and Bolts

Initial consultation - 150.00 plus applicable taxes (which you can put towards my services @ 50.00 per hour)

Hourly rate - 50.00 plus applicable taxes. I charge for travel time if in access of 1/2 hour, and for time sourcing or procuring materials and furnishing for your project. An initial deposit is required on large scale projects and is based on the estimated total cost.

I can work with your contractor or recommend someone to you.

I pass discounts on to you, but apply a 10% surcharge on anything that I purchase for your project.
